I-Compete

Date: 10/12/2016

Location:

Recorded By: Samya Hatem

  • Description:
    • iCompete is a technical competition that is designed to bring out your inner innovator. and offers you the chance to reach your fullest technical potentials by offering a wide variety of track-based competitions designed specifically to challenge, test & evaluate your skills in all respects.
    • iCompete Focuses on helping you implement your technical knowledge into solving real life problems that are faced by industry leaders. Facing these challenges will help you take the step you need to become a true engineer
    • iCompete can be considered as a selection phase for the internships program so, Challenges are determined and judged by our industrial partners.
      • There was an Info session and our Speaker in it was:Dr. Maged Ghoneima
      The Link of the info session
  • The Main Event
    i-Compete is a technical contest hosted by i-Hub & IEEE ASU branch targeting 1st ,2nd and 3rd year students. I-Compete consists of 9 different tracks, each one has a contest determined and judged by our industrial partners. These tracks are:
    • 1- Energy Track.
    • 2- Communication Track.
    • 3- Embedded Track.
    • 4- Software Track.
    • 5- Mechatronics Track.
    • 6- Electronic Devices Track.
    • 7- Mechanical Design Track.
    • 8- Electronic Circuits Track.
    • 9- Architecture Track.

IEEE ASU SB Recruitment

Date: 28/9/2016

Location:

Recorded By: Samya Hatem

Number of attendees: 200

Number of volunteers: 70

  • Description:
    • IEEE ASU SB has taken the initiation and the commitment to spread technological awareness and improve both technical and non-technical skills of Egyptian students as well as working on developing scientific research in Egypt as a part of a smart plan to achieve total development of Egypt. IEEE ASU SB was recruiting to join the largest professional organization for the advancement of technology, and help the SB us in creating a bright future for Egypt. IEEE ASU SB was recruiting for six committees: PR, HR, FR, L&R, IT and Media.
  • Target:
    • IEEE ASU SB was searching for friendly, committed, and hard worker volunteers to help the branch to spread technological awareness and improve both technical and non-technical skills of Egyptian students as well as working on developing scientific research in Egypt.
  • Output::
    • 70 students had joined the IEEE ASU SB, and willing to do their best to improve and develop our branch
